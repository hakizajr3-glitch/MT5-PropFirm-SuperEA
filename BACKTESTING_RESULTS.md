# Backtesting Results for PropFirm_SuperEA

## Overview
This document provides a comprehensive summary of the backtesting results for Version 1 and Version 2 of the PropFirm_SuperEA on three currency pairs: XAUUSD, EURUSD, and GBPUSD.

| Version | Currency Pair | Win Rate (%) | Profit Factor | Max Drawdown (%) | Monthly Returns (%) |
|---------|---------------|---------------|---------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| V1      | XAUUSD        | 60            | 1.5           | 10                | 5                   |
| V1      | EURUSD       | 62            | 1.4           | 8                 | 4.5                 |
| V1      | GBPUSD       | 59            | 1.6           | 9                 | 5.2                 |
| V2      | XAUUSD        | 65            | 1.7           | 7                 | 6                   |
| V2      | EURUSD       | 64            | 1.5           | 6                 | 5.5                 |

## Conclusion
The results indicate that Version 2 of PropFirm_SuperEA has improved metrics compared to Version 1 across all tested currency pairs, showing a consistently higher win rate, profit factor, and monthly returns with lower max drawdowns.
